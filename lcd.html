Display Options: PCB-Mounted vs. External

Your converter’s form factor and use case dictate the display. We offer two powerhouse options—**PCB-mounted** and **external**—each packed with customization to fit your vision.

PCB-Mounted LCD Displays: Compact Power Monitoring

For tight spaces and seamless integration, our **PCB-mounted LCD displays** are soldered right onto your DC-DC converter’s board. These aren’t just screens—they’re miniature command centers.

  • **Sizes**: From 0.66-inch OLEDs (128x64 pixels) for ultra-compact designs to 2-inch TFTs for richer data layouts.
  • **Display Types**:
    • **OLED**: High contrast, low power, perfect for battery-driven converters.
    • **Segment Displays**: Simple, rugged, ideal for single-parameter readouts like output voltage.
    • **TFT**: Full-color, graphical interfaces for multi-rail monitoring or efficiency curves.
  • **Customization**:
    • Data fields: Show input/output voltage, current, wattage, or efficiency percentages.
    • Layouts: Numeric grids, bar graphs, or custom logos etched into the startup screen.
    • Interfaces: I2C for simplicity, SPI for speed—your call.
  • **Power Draw**: As low as 5mA, sipping from your converter’s output to keep efficiency intact.
  • **Mounting**: Low-profile headers or surface-mount designs, aligned to your PCB’s footprint.
  • **Use Cases**: IoT nodes, wearable tech, or any converter where the PCB is king.

**Why It Rocks**: Zero external wiring, total integration, and a sleek look that screams “professional.”

**Trade-Off**: Fixed to the PCB, so it’s best where the board’s accessible or visible.

Core Technology: Microcontrollers & Precision Sensing

Our LCD displays aren’t just pretty faces—they’re powered by **microcontrollers** and **precision sensing** that make your DC-DC converters talk. Here’s the tech that drives the magic:

Microcontrollers: The Heartbeat of Your Display

Every display runs on a **microcontroller**—a tiny brain that pulls data, crunches numbers, and updates your screen faster than you can blink.

  • **Capabilities**:
    • Reads voltage/current sensor outputs via 12-bit or 16-bit ADCs (0.001V resolution).
    • Calculates wattage (P = V × I) and efficiency (P_out / P_in × 100%) in real time.
    • Drives the LCD with crisp updates—up to 60Hz for smooth visuals.
  • **Options**:
    • **Low-Power**: STM32L0 for battery-driven converters (sleep currents < 1µA).
    • **High-Performance**: STM32F4 for multi-rail monitoring or advanced features.
  • **Firmware**:
    • Custom-coded in C or C++—your specs, our execution.
    • Features like overcurrent alerts, data logging to onboard flash, or I2C master-slave comms.
  • **Integration**: Sits on your PCB or in the external display, sipping minimal power (e.g., 10mA at 3.3V).

**Why It Matters**: It’s not just data—it’s actionable intelligence, tailored to your converter’s quirks.

Precision Sensing: Voltage, Current, and Wattage Mastery

Great displays need great data. Our **sensing suite** ensures your DC-DC converter’s vitals are tracked with surgical precision.

Voltage Sensing

We measure input and output voltages with resolution that catches every ripple.

  • **Method**: Voltage dividers with 0.1% resistors or INA219 chips for high-side sensing.
  • **Range**: 1V to 100V (or higher with custom scaling).
  • **Accuracy**: ±0.5% typical, ±0.1% with calibration—down to 1mV resolution.
  • **Noise Filtering**: Onboard capacitors and software averaging kill ripple noise.

Current Sensing

From microamps to amps, we’ve got your current covered—input and output.

  • **Techniques**:
    • **Shunt Resistors**: 1mΩ to 100mΩ, paired with amplifiers (e.g., INA226) for ±0.5% accuracy.
    • **Hall Effect**: ACS712 or custom modules for isolated, high-current sensing (up to 50A).
  • **Range**: 10µA to 50A+, matched to your converter’s load.
  • **Calibration**: Factory-tuned to your design for zero drift.

Wattage & Efficiency

Wattage is where it all comes together—live power readings that tell you everything.

  • **Calculation**: Microcontroller multiplies voltage and current every millisecond.
  • **Display**: Raw watts (e.g., “15.32 W”), efficiency percentages, or time-averaged trends.
  • **Extras**: Overpower warnings, peak logging, or efficiency curves over load ranges.

**Why It Matters**: Your converter’s performance isn’t a mystery—it’s a story told in real time.
